Certified Pre-Owned (CPO) vehicles undergo a rigorous multi-point inspection to ensure quality and reliability. Inspection criteria may vary based on manufacturer guidelines. Warranty coverage, when provided, applies to specific components for a defined period. Roadside assistance may be included with select CPO vehicles, subject to the terms of the program. For detailed warranty information, refer to the documentation provided with the purchase of the CPO vehicle.

Trade-in values are determined based on the vehicle’s condition, mileage, year, and market demand. Not all vehicles qualify for trade-in credit. Trade-in values may be applied toward the purchase of a new or pre-owned vehicle or offered as dealership credit. A valid government-issued ID is required for all trade-ins. Certain restrictions apply, and terms may vary between online and in-store trade-in programs. The dealership reserves the right to refuse or adjust trade-in offers at its discretion. Additional details are available from our trade-in partner.

Financing is available to qualified applicants and is subject to credit approval by participating lenders. Eligibility criteria, interest rates, and loan terms depend on creditworthiness, vehicle selection, and lender policies. Financing options may vary and are only available for qualifying applicants. Additional terms and conditions may apply. Contact our financing team for more details.

Test drives are available upon request and require a valid driver’s license. Test drive availability is subject to local regulations and vehicle inventory. Certain restrictions may apply, including limitations on distance or duration. Please inquire with the dealership for specific test drive terms.

Optional extended warranties and maintenance plans are available for purchase on select vehicles. Terms, conditions, and exclusions apply and may vary by plan and vehicle type. Routine maintenance services are performed by certified technicians using manufacturer-approved parts to ensure the highest standards of care.

Certain features, services, and offers may not be available in all regions. Availability is determined by local laws, dealership capabilities, and program terms. Please contact your local dealership for details about regional availability and eligibility.

Terms and conditions are subject to change without notice. For questions about warranty details, trade-in restrictions, financing options, or other policies, visit our website or contact the dealership directly. Warranty information, trade-in requirements, and financing terms are provided at the time of purchase.
